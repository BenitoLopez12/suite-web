Steps to apply the license:

1. Login to Remedy Smart Reporting using “siadmin” account into the “Default” organization through the application direct URL:
•	Bundled Tomcat: http(s)://SmartReporting_host:<port>
•	External Tomcat: http(s)://SmartReporting_host:<port>/SmartReporting
2. Navigate to Administration > Admin Console and click on “Licence Management”
3. Click on “Upload Licence” to expand the options and click on “Browse” button to select the license included on this file
4. Click on “Preview” button to verify the new license information and click on “Submit” button
